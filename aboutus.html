About Us

Welcome to the official website of "Scooped," a thrilling new film that takes you on a suspenseful journey through the dark alleys of power, corruption, and the relentless pursuit of truth. Here, we celebrate the creative minds and talented artists who brought this gripping story to life.

Embark on an Epic Journey

Embark on an epic journey with three brave kids as they uncover a thrilling secret in their local park! "Scooped" is a heart-pounding adventure that combines the innocence of childhood with the excitement of a high-stakes mystery. When best friends Max, Lily, and Jack discover strange activities in their neighborhood park, their curiosity leads them to a hidden stash of stolen goods. Realizing they’ve stumbled upon a devious plot by a group of clever thieves, the trio decides to take matters into their own hands. Armed with quick wits and boundless courage, they embark on a mission to outsmart the culprits and reclaim the stolen loot.

A Thrilling Adventure

As they follow the clues, the kids face numerous challenges and dangerous encounters, testing their friendship and bravery. Along the way, they uncover not just the mystery of the thieves, but also hidden strengths within themselves. Their journey is filled with suspense, humor, and the sweet taste of victory that comes from unexpected flavors. "Scooped" is a delightful blend of mystery, adventure, and heartwarming moments that will captivate audiences of all ages. The film showcases the power of friendship and the thrill of adventure, making it a perfect family movie night choice.

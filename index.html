Scooped

A Riveting New Thriller Unveiling the Dark Underbelly of Power

Embark on an epic journey with three brave kids as they uncover a thrilling secret in their local park! "Scooped" is a heart-pounding adventure that combines the innocence of childhood with the excitement of a high-stakes mystery. When best friends Max, Lily, and Jack discover strange activities in their neighborhood park, their curiosity leads them to a hidden stash of stolen goods. Realizing they’ve stumbled upon a devious plot by a group of clever thieves, the trio decides to take matters into their own hands. Armed with quick wits and boundless courage, they embark on a mission to outsmart the culprits and reclaim the stolen loot. As they follow the clues, the kids face numerous challenges and dangerous encounters, testing their friendship and bravery. Along the way, they uncover not just the mystery of the thieves, but also hidden strengths within themselves.

Characters

Racheal

Age: 10
Role: Team Member, Strategic Thinker

Background: Rachel is Payton’s newest friend and quickly becomes a vital part of the team, known for her clever ideas and strategic thinking. Her intelligence and resourcefulness often help the group navigate complex challenges and devise effective plans to stay close to the thieves.

Personality:Rachel is smart, analytical, and always thinking several steps ahead. Her calm demeanor and quick problem-solving skills balance out the more impulsive nature of her friends. Caden

Brandon

Age: 10
Role: Team Member, Comic Relief

Background: Brandon is one of Payton’s close friends and a key member of the team working to stop the thieves. Known for his sense of humor and light-hearted approach to problem-solving, Brandon provides much-needed comic relief during their various escapades.

Personality:Brandon is witty, playful, and always ready, even in the face of danger. His light-heartedness helps keep the team’s spirits high, though his impulsiveness sometimes lands them in tricky situations. Despite this, he’s fiercely loyal to his friends and always ready to give someone a lift.

Payton

Age: 10
Role: Protagonist, Quasi Leader

Background: Payton is the brave and resourceful quasi-leader of a small group of friends determined to stop a band of thieves. Despite being only 10 years old, Payton's natural can-do attitude and quick thinking guide the team through a series of misadventures and blunders as they take on the criminals.

Personality:Confident and determined, Payton takes charge in the face of danger but isn’t afraid to admit when things go wrong. With a mix of bravery and youthful enthusiasm, Payton navigates the group's challenges with a positive outlook and a willingness to learn from mistakes.

Sigs

Role: Criminal Associate

Background: Sigs is the middle brother in the criminal trio led by Karel. He’s constantly trying to prove his worth but often ends up complicating the plans with his harebrained schemes and misguided attempts to take charge. His loyalty to his brothers is unwavering, even if his execution leaves much to be desired.

Personality: Sigs is ambitious and impulsive, with a penchant for getting himself and others into sticky situations. His enthusiasm is infectious, but his lack of foresight often turns minor hiccups into major setbacks. Despite this, he has a good heart and always means well.

Reggie

Role: Criminal Associate, Ice Cream Driver

Background: Reggie is the youngest of Karel’s brothers, often finding himself in over his head with the criminal schemes his family concocts. Despite his best efforts, he tends to create more chaos than order, earning him a reputation for being the “wild card” of the group.

Personality: Reggie is eager but often clueless, bringing a mix of enthusiasm and unintentional comedy to the team. He looks up to his older brothers and wants to prove himself, but his knack for messing things up usually gets in the way. His unpredictable nature adds a layer of unpredictability to every heist.

Krisina

Role : Hacker
Age : 24

Background: Krisina is a highly skilled and disciplined. She is known for her technical expertise and no-nonsense attitude. She plays a crucial role in the heist, using her coding skills and strategic mind to infiltrate security systems and ensure smooth operations.

Personality: Professional and meticulous, Krisina thrives on precision and efficiency. She prefers to work alone, believing that others often slow her down. Her sharp wit and ability to stay calm under pressure make her an invaluable asset to the team.

Karel

Occupation: Criminal Mastermind
Role: Protagonist

Background: Meet Karel, the not-so-lucky criminal mastermind with a name that’s often mistaken for a girl’s. Don’t let the name fool you, though—Karel’s got the brains, the skills, and the sarcasm to pull off the biggest heist of his life. If only he weren’t constantly surrounded by, as he puts it, “idiots.”

Personality: Karel is a mix of cunning and exasperation, always one step ahead of the authorities but perpetually three steps behind thanks to his brothers. He’s got a dry wit and a sarcastic edge, often using humor to mask his frustration. Despite his criminal ways, you can’t help but root for him as he tries to wrangle his siblings and pull off his grand heist.
